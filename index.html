Our mission:

It is our goal to help average people become more politically knowledgable, active, and engaged by making the political realm accessible and easy to understand.

We believe that a more politically educated and engaged population leads to a more effective and transparent government.

Greater political activity by citizens encourages politicians to embrace public voices, outreach, and efforts rather than any influences other than their constituents.
